<p>The abstract is a summary of your report, with parts from each section. Even though it is at the front of the report, you should write it last, because you do not know what you need to summarize until the rest has been written. </p>
<p>In 200-300 words or less, summarize the study&#39;s main objectives, give the scientific name of the organism you studied, and the hypothesis you are testing. Summarize the background, methods used, main results, and main conclusions. You should not include references in your abstract.</p>
